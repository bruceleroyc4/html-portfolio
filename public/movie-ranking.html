The Best Movies According to Bruce

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Last Dragon - 1985

You had to have been there. 1980s, New York, Rap and R&B, and proper homage to Bruce Lee. How can you go wrong?

RAD - 1986

Cult classic! True underdog story that tugs at the heartstrings with some classic 1980s motivational montage music. It's Rocky on a bicycle.

A Few Good Men - 1992
